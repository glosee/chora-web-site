HTML (HyperText Markup Language) is a standard developed over the years to convey information
over the internet by using "hyperlinks" - or just links as we know them today.
As opposed to a PDF, an HTML page is much more dynamic in nature,
letting you browse the web by clicking on links and interacting with the page.
Links could take you either to a different location within the current page,
or to a different page over the internet.
